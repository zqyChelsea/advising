When it's heavy right now

If you need help tonight, this hour, this minute

There's always a real person ready to listen. Please reach out — even if you're not sure what to say.

Office hours · 9am–6pm

SAO Counsellors

Walk in to QT308, Chow Yei Ching Building (Core T entrance). Or call ahead — counsellors will see you for an urgent appointment.

📞 Call2766 6800
Evenings, weekends, holidays

PolyU-LINE Hotline

Mon–Fri 6pm to 9am, plus 24 hours on weekends and public holidays. Answered by Vital Employee Service Consultancy & followed up by SAO counsellors.

🌙 Call8100 1583
Immediate emergency

Campus Security · 24/7

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call campus security right away. For life-threatening emergencies, also call 999.

🚨 Call2766 7999
